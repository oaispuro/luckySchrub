Our History

Lucky Schrub was started by Lorraine and Bill Jacobson, a family born and raised in Tucson, Az. Landscaping began as a hobby the Jacobson marriage dedicated time and effort to, but after winning neighborhood competitions and getting the recognition of local media, the couple was contacted about prividing landscaping services which became quickly a hit getting important projects like remodeling the new City Hall and the modeling of very important businesses.
